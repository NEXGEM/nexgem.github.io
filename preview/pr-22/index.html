Research Focus

Three themes shaping our work

Our lab is organized around questions that move from biological mechanism to clinically actionable insight.

Synthetic lethality

We identify targetable genetic interactions that can guide combination therapies and precision cancer treatment strategies.

Spatial omics and pathology

We combine histopathology, spatial omics, and machine learning to reconstruct tumor ecosystems with greater resolution.

Translational AI

We build models that bridge patient-scale molecular data and real-world therapeutic decisions in oncology.
